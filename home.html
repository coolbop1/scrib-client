About Us

    Welcome to scrib, your number one source for all things electronic books. We're dedicated to providing you the very best of electronic library, with an emphasis on Novels, Personal notes library, ebooks market hub, and hand-writen to digital text conversion.

   Founded in 2017 by Bidemi Oritunmise Peter , scrib has come a long way from its beginnings in Ladoke Akintola university of technology (Lautech) Ogbomosho, Nigeria. When Bidemi first started out, his passion for "eco-friendly reading materials" and "preserving valuable educational materials" drove them to start their own business.

   We hope you enjoy our products as much as we enjoy offering them to you. If you have any questions or comments, please don't hesitate to contact us.
